    Richard "Rick" Sanchez, also known as Rick C-137, is the titular main protagonist of Rick and Morty. He is a genius scientist whose alcoholism and reckless, nihilistic behavior are a source of concern for his daughter's family, as well as the safety of their son, Morty.
